# Emergency Support Resources

## Crisis Situations

If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself:

1. Call emergency services immediately.
2. Contact a trusted family member or friend.
3. Visit the nearest hospital emergency department.
4. Contact university support services.

## Malaysia Emergency Numbers

* Emergency: 999
* Ambulance: 999
* Police: 999

## University Support

Students are encouraged to contact:

* MMU Student Affairs Division (STAD)
* MMU Counselling Unit
* Faculty advisors
* Academic mentors

## Warning Signs

* Thoughts of self-harm
* Statements about wanting to die
* Extreme hopelessness
* Severe emotional distress
* Social withdrawal

## Important Notice

This chatbot does not provide medical diagnosis or therapy. It serves as a first-line support and referral system.
