# MMU University Support

## Student Affairs Division (STAD)

Student Affairs Division supports:

* Student welfare
* Student activities
* Counselling referrals
* Campus engagement

## Academic Support

Students may seek assistance from:

* Academic advisors
* Faculty lecturers
* Programme coordinators
* Examination and Records Unit (ERU)

## Student Development Workshops

Topics may include:

* Motivation
* Communication skills
* Interview preparation
* Resume writing
* Study skills
* Personal development

## Campus Resources

* Library services
* Learning support
* Student clubs and societies
* Career development programmes

For the latest information, students should refer to official MMU announcements and student portals.
