# Academic Support

## Managing Assignment Stress

- Create a weekly study plan.
- Prioritize deadlines.
- Break projects into smaller tasks.

## Exam Preparation

- Revise consistently.
- Practice past-year questions.
- Study with peers when helpful.

## Time Management

- Use a calendar.
- Set realistic goals.
- Avoid last-minute work.

## Seeking Academic Help

Students may contact:
- Lecturers
- Tutors
- Academic advisors
- Faculty support staff
