# Frequently Asked Questions

## I feel stressed. What should I do?
Try taking a short break, speaking with someone you trust, and organizing your tasks into smaller goals.

## Can this chatbot replace a counsellor?
No. The chatbot provides support and guidance but does not replace professional counselling.

## Is my conversation confidential?
Conversations should be handled according to university privacy policies.

## I am worried about my grades.
Consider discussing your concerns with your lecturer, academic advisor, or faculty representative.

## I feel lonely at university.
Try joining student clubs, participating in campus activities, or speaking with a counsellor.
