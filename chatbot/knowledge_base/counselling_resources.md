# MMU Student Counselling Resources

## About Counselling Services

The MMU Counselling Unit provides support for students experiencing personal, emotional, academic, or career-related challenges.

Services include:

* Personal counselling
* Educational counselling
* Career counselling
* Group counselling
* Student development workshops
* Support for students with special needs

## Common Issues Students Face

* Academic stress
* Exam anxiety
* Time management difficulties
* Lack of motivation
* Homesickness
* Relationship concerns
* Career uncertainty
* Self-confidence issues

## Stress Management Tips

1. Break large tasks into smaller goals.
2. Maintain a study schedule.
3. Sleep at least 7–8 hours daily.
4. Exercise regularly.
5. Seek support from friends, family, or counsellors.

## Self-Care Checklist

* Have I eaten properly today?
* Have I slept enough?
* Have I taken breaks?
* Have I spoken to someone I trust?
* Have I completed one small goal today?

## When To Seek Professional Help

Consider contacting a counsellor if:

* Stress affects daily activities.
* You experience persistent sadness.
* You struggle to attend classes.
* You feel overwhelmed for an extended period.
* You have thoughts of harming yourself.
